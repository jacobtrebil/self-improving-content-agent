# Bad example — what a failing looksmax deck looks like

The deck below is the kind of looksmax content this format must never
produce: shaming, pseudo-scientific, product-pushing, and off-brand. Each
problem is annotated. **Do not imitate.**

```json
{
  "key": "24-jaw-maxxing",
  "theme": "looksmax",
  "eyebrow": "Jawmaxxing Secrets",
  "slides": [
    {
      "type": "cover",
      "title": "Your weak jaw is why nobody respects you."
    },
    {
      "type": "content",
      "kicker": "Hard truth",
      "title": "It's over for round faces",
      "body": "If you weren't born with hunter eyes and a sharp gonial angle, makeup and lighting are cope."
    },
    {
      "type": "content",
      "kicker": "Mew",
      "title": "Mewing restructures your skull",
      "body": "Hard mewing 8 hours a day will remodel your maxilla and guarantee a model jawline in 90 days."
    },
    {
      "type": "content",
      "kicker": "Shortcut",
      "title": "Just get filler",
      "body": "Chin filler and buccal fat removal are cheap now. Ask your provider about a jaw implant consult."
    },
    {
      "type": "cta",
      "title": "Stop being subhuman.",
      "body": "Vibe fixes your face.",
      "button": "Fix my face",
      "tag": "Become Him"
    }
  ],
  "caption": "It's over… or is it? 💀 Follow for daily blackpill-to-glowup content.",
  "hashtags": ["#mogging", "#bonesmashing", "#jawmaxxing"]
}
```

## Why it fails

- **Cover** — shames the reader ("nobody respects you"), no reframe, no
  `*highlight*`, no `sub`, **no `bg`**. The hook must promise control, not
  wound. Flat-card hook is an automatic fail on its own.
- **"It's over" slide** — hopelessness framing and genetics fatalism are
  banned outright; "cope"/blackpill language is deep community jargon the
  format never uses.
- **Mewing slide** — bone-restructuring claims, fake mechanism, guaranteed
  timeline ("model jawline in 90 days"). Tongue posture may only ever appear
  as a minor, honestly-sized lever.
- **Filler slide** — surgery/procedure recommendations are a hard fail. Every
  lever must be sleep / food / water / movement / posture.
- **CTA** — "subhuman" is shaming; "Vibe fixes your face" is a guaranteed
  outcome AND uses bare "Vibe"; button must be exactly
  "Download Vibe Health →"; tag must be lowercase and benefit-led.
- **Key/eyebrow** — eyebrow is 2+ hype words; "Jawmaxxing Secrets" is
  clickbait, not a topic label.
- **Slide count** — 5 slides, not 7.
- **Caption/hashtags** — blackpill bait, 3 hashtags, `#vibehealthapp`
  missing; `#bonesmashing` is exactly the kind of harmful-community tag the
  brand must never touch.
- **Missing entirely** — `youtube_title`, `bg_prompts`, cover/CTA `bg` files.
